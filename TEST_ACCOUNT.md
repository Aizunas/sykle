3. **Map** — explore nearby partner cafés and bakeries, tap pins to see details
4. **Partners** — tap a partner, add a reward to your basket
5. **Basket** — review your rewards, swipe to generate a voucher
6. **Wallet** — view your generated vouchers and QR codes
7. **Leaderboard** — see the weekly CO₂ rankings
8. **Profile** — check your sykles balance, stats, and settings
9. **Customer support** — chat with the AI assistant

- HealthKit and GPS do not work in the browser version — use a real iPhone for those features
- The backend is live on Railway — no local setup needed
